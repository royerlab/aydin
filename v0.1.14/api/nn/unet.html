UNetModel

class aydin.nn.models.unet.UNetModel(*args, **kwargs)[source]

Standard UNet model. Three training modes are available: supervised: noisy and clean images are required, shiftconv: self-supervised learning with shift and conv scheme non-shiftconv: self-supervised learning by masking pixels at each iteration
